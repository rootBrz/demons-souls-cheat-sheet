Tutorial

  • From the start head down the passages and corridors past the Dreglings to some stairs to grab Crescent Moon Grass
  • Continue to a bridge and on your left will be Crescent Moon Grass x3
  • Drop down into the marshy area and continue to a gazebo-like structure to find an Archstone. Touch with it to be taken to the next area
  • Continue forward through the halls and past the Blue Eye Knights to your left behind some barrels is Half Moon Grass
  • Climb the stairs and on the left will be an Unknown Soldier's Soul
  • Take the right path and climb the stairs past the forge. Pass the fog gate and take a right down some stairs to grab Half Moon Grass x2
  • Turn around and head back up the stairs and continue up the stairs to another fog gate you can cross. Defeat Vanguard; acquire Grey Demon's Soul
  • Touch with the Archstone to be taken to the next area
  • At the end of the corridor before the stairs you can grab Full Moon Grass
  • Facing the dragon, take the left stairs to grab Full Moon Grass
  • Take a left behind you and to your right behind a pillar is Full Moon Grass x2
  • Turn around to grab Shard of Hardstone x3
  • In the next room is Shard of Sharpstone x3
  • Next is a Renowned Soldier's Soul
  • And another Renowned Soldier's Soul
  • And one more Renowned Soldier's Soul
  • Continue forward to be sucker-punched into the Nexus

The Nexus

  • If you died to Vanguard you will be taken here
  • Talk to Blacksmith Boldwin
  • Talk to Stockpile Thomas
  • Talk to the Crestfallen Warrior
  • Touch the Archstone of the Small King and warp to the Boletarian Palace

Boletarian Palace

  • Upon being transported, turn around and behind planks you will find Late Moon Grass x2
  • Head towards the palace
  • If you have Pure White or Black World Tendency, there's a door to your left that'll be open
  • Head through to an area that'll have several corpses with a total of five Unknown Soldier's Soul
  • Here will also be a Crystal Lizard that drops Shard of Sharpstone x6
  • Head up the hillside to find a corpse with a Renowned Soldier's Soul
  • If you have Pure Black World Tendency, head into the tower to find Black Phantom Executioner Miralda; kill her for a Guillotine Axe
  • If you have Pure White World Tendency, head into the tower to find Executioner Miralda; kill her for the Binded Cross Set + Master's Ring
  • Head down the tower stairs to find the Ring of Poison Resistance
  • Here will be a broken beam you can drop down through to pick up a Colorless Demon's Soul
  • Drop all the way down to find the Brushwood Set + Large Brushwood Shield
  • Continue up the stairs past the fountain and take a left. Pass through a doorway to enter a room, then continue to another room with beds. Here will be a staircase and to the right behind breakable shelves will be Firebomb x5
  • Head up the stairs and to your right will be a fog gate. Pass through it and to your right will be an Unknown Soldier's Soul and a bit further will be a Mail Breaker
  • Continue upward to some scaffolding then take a left to end up outside. At the top you can take a left through a doorway. Take a left to grab Half Moon Grass x2
  • Head up the stairs where the trap is. Where it breaks part of the scaffolding will be a Bastard Sword
  • Head up the stairs to a rampart. Take the right behind you and at the end you will find Half Moon Grass x4
  • Head into the tower and take a left out onto another rampart with a Red Eye Knight guarding an Unknown Hero's Soul
  • Head back onto the rampart with catapults and head past the Blue Eye Knight into a tower with Firebomb x6
  • Head down the stairs here and take a left eventually to find a balcony with chains you can break. Continue down the tower to find a lever you can pull to open a shortcut and grab the Cling Ring
  • To your right outside you can grab the Old Raggedy Set + Old Spice
  • Here you can also grab the Jade Hair Ornament. You may give this Ornamanet to Stockpile Thomas for a Ring of Herculean Strength
  • Head back up the tower and onto the rampart and cross the fog gate on your right. Take a right down some stairs and take a left. Take a right then another right and destroy some wooden planks to reach a room with the Dregling Merchant
  • Head back to the base of the stairs we just descended and parallel to them you can walk over the wall to an area with the Thief's Ring
  • Talk to Ostrava, of Boletaria. He will ask you to kill the Dreglings just below you. Do so and he will jump down afterwards and you may talk to him to receive the Brass Telescope
  • Grab the Unknown Soldier's Soul from here as well
  • Continue forward and take a right from the tower where the Boletaria Soldiers jump from. To your left in the side corridor is a Scimitar
  • Continue to the right into an armory. Head down the wooden stairs and grab Shard of Hardstone x3 + Shard of Sharpstone x3
  • Head back up the wooden stairs and behind a small corner will be an Unknown Hero's Soul
  • Head back and head into the hallway past the Blue Eye Knight. To your left will be an Unknown Soldier's Soul
  • Continue forward and take a right to break through some shelves. Head up the stairs here and take a right up some more stairs. Take a right at the top onto some scaffolding to pick up an Unknown Soldier's Soul
  • Continue up the stone stairs and to your left on some scaffolding will be Late Moon Grass x2
  • Turn around and pass through the fog gate. Continue forward and take a right past the trap. At the end take a right up some stairs and continue forward to the rampart with the Red Dragon. To you right at the end you can pick up an Unknown Soldier's Soul
  • Head across and just inside the other side is a lever you can pull to open the main gate to the Palace
  • Turn around and head forward outside. Take a left and break through some shelves to reach Turpentine x4 at the top of the stairs
  • Turn around and head down the stairs. Take a right then a left then another right into a room with some tables. Head down the stairs past the Hoplite and take a right outside to grab an Unknown Hero's Soul
  • Continue down the tower and at the bottom you can pick up Half Moon Grass x3
  • Pull the lever to open the shortcut
  • Head outside and to your right will be a fog gate leading to the boss fight. Defeat Phalanx; acquire Lead Demon's Soul
  • Touch with the Archstone to return to the Nexus
  • Talk to the Maiden in Black and she will give you the Blue Eye Stone + White Eye Stone. Talk to her again and agree to 'Seek Soul Power'. She will allow you to level up via Souls
  • Head up five sets of stairs and on a walkway you can talk to the Monumental. Accept the mission
  • If you have Pure White Character Tendency, you may talk to the Monumental to get the Friend's Ring
  • Continue to the stairs to the right of the Monumental and head down. At the base of the stairs on your left will be a Stone of Ephemeral Eyes

Stonefang Tunnel

  • Touch the Archstone of the Burrow King and warp to Stonefang Tunnel
  • Upon being transported, take the stairs up and head left to kill a Crystal Lizard for Shard of Sharpstone x8
  • Continue forward to grab at the ledge an Unknown Hero's Soul
  • Turn around and head across the bridge past the stairs that lead to the Archstone. Head into the tower and to the right will be a lever you can turn. Head out the doorway here and hop onto the lift going upward. Hop off onto the next platform with a doorway. Enter and on a balcony behind the Fat Official is a Crushing Battle Axe+1
  • In the room is also a Renowned Soldier's Soul
  • Head back outside and take the lift down to pick up a Stone of Ephemeral Eyes
  • If your World Tendency is just above Pure Black, there will be a Primeval Demon that will spawn here that may drop a Colorless Demon's Soul
  • Hop back on the lift and drop off onto the first platform that leads back to where we activated the lift. Continue forward to a room with a Fat Official. Behind him will be another room with a Chunk of Hardstone + Chunk of Sharpstone
  • Pick up Shard of Clearstone x4 from here as well
  • Turn around and head back into the previous room. To the side will be a small doorway leading to a tunnel. Climb the stairs here to pick up Crescent Moon Grass x4 at the corner
  • Continue forward and to your right will be a room with a Pickaxe
  • Head out of the tunnel to a wooden walkway and take a left. Climb the stone stairs and on a corpse near a bonfire will be an Unknown Soldier's Soul
  • Head back down the stone stairs and stick to the left side of the wooden bridge to avoid falling when it breaks. Head to the other side with the Fat Official and grab a Renowned Hero's Soul
  • Drop down to the stone plateau and pull the lever to open a gate
  • Continue to a wooden bridge and cross it. Continue across the stone walkway and drop down onto a slightly lower level to pick up an Unknown Soldier's Soul
  • Drop down again and head through the tunnel. Go up the stone stairs and cross the stone walkway to the gate we opened. Pass through the fog gate. Take a right past the Fire Lizard to grab a Shard of Meltstone
  • Head up the wooden ramp and take a left. Head up a wooden ramp and at the top you can pick up Half Moon Grass x2
  • Press on and take a right into a circular room with Fire Lizards and a Shard of Dragonstone
  • Exit and continue forward. Continue past the fog gate on your left and head down the slope. Head outside and on your right on the ledge will be an Unknown Hero's Soul
  • Turn around and on the left of the bridge will be Crescent Moon Grass x2
  • Head across the wooden platform and turn the lever to start the lift
  • Halfway down on your left will be a Shard of Clearstone
  • Head to the bottom and drop off. Press a bit forward to a wooden platform with a Renowned Hero's Soul
  • Go back inside and talk to Blacksmith Ed. He will allow you to make Weapons and various Items from Demon Souls
  • Take the lift all the way back up and head back to the sloping tunnel. Take the fog gate on your right and head down the short tunnel to reach a forge-like area. On your left behind a furnace will be a Large Hardstone Shard
  • Continue forward back into the large hall. Head down some wooden stairs and sprint across the wooden board to reach an area with two Unknown Soldier's Soul and a Great Club. Note that if you fail to cross the board before it breaks you will have to reload the area to reach the items again
  • Drop down onto the narrow ledge on your left then onto the floor. Head up the wooden ramps and make your way through the fog gate tunnel and out to the hall. Take a right and roll through several barrels and climb the stairs behind them. Up here you can grab Crescent Moon Grass x2
  • At the end corner on your right will be a ledge you can drop onto to pick up an Unknown Hero's Soul
  • Drop down onto the crates to grab a Steel Shield
  • Run across the wooden bridge here and go up the stairs. Continue all the way to the large stone with Fire Lizards. Turn left to grab a Shard of Dragonstone
  • Turn right here and take a left down the ramp. Around the corner to your left will be Ed's Grindstone x2
  • Turn around and head to the bridge past the ramp we just descended. Down the first ramp will be Half Moon Grass
  • Continue down and take a left behind you. On your left you can pick up an Unknown Hero's Soul
  • Just ahead is a lever you can turn to stop the waterwheel and open a path ahead
  • Head forward and drop down. Take a right and head up the wooden ramp. Head past the circular room with Fire Lizards and drop down on the right to pick up a Ring of Poison Resistance
  • Turn around and head through the busted cell bars. Here you can pick up Half Moon Grass x4 and a Large Sharpstone Shard
  • Head up the stairs where the Grass was and go all the way to the buckets. Take a right and go down the stairs to a tunnel with several explosive barrels. Head through the fog gate behind the Fat Official and to your right behind the wagons you can grab a Stone of Ephemeral Eyes
  • Head across the wooden walkway and head up the wooden ramp. Take a left and head up the wooden ramp to your left. Take a left and jump across the gap to reach a Kris Blade
  • Drop down and turn the lever in front of you to tower the platform you are on
  • Facing the fog gate, behind you to your left is a tunnel where you can pick up a Chunk of Spiderstone
  • Go through the fog gate. Defeat Armor Spider; acquire Hard Demon's Soul + Pure Spiderstone
  • Here you can also pick up a Renowned Soldier's Soul and a Chunk of Spiderstone

Tower of Latria

  • Touch the Archstone of the Tower Queen to be taken to the Tower of Latria
  • Head out onto the walkway and take a right to find Augite of Guidance x3
  • Turn around and head down the walkway. Take a right and on the wall is the Prison of Hope, 3F Cell Key. Use this key to unlock the first cell behind you and pick up the Wizard's Set + Wooden Catalyst
  • To your left behind the coffins is a Renowned Soldier's Soul
  • Head right down the walkway and at the end to your right is a Renowned Soldier's Soul
  • To your left in the cell is a Shard of Mercurystone
  • Head back down the walkway and to your right will be a corridor. Turn right at the end and open the first cell to pick up a Shard of Mercurystone
  • Turn around and in the second cell after the Dregling in the corner will be a Silver Coronet
  • Continue down the walkway and on the right on the wall will be Prison of Hope, 3F Key
  • Turn right and in the third cell on the left will be a Shard of Mercurystone
  • At the end of this walkway is a Renowned Soldier's Soul
  • Head all the way back to the walkway with the Archstone and at the end is a locked gate you can now open
  • Pass through the fog gate. Head up the stairs and open the first cell on your left. Here you can open the coffin to pick up a Stone of Ephemeral Eyes
  • Just behind that is an Unknown Hero's Soul
  • Continue down the walkway then take a right. Open the first cell on your left to grab Fresh Spice
  • Continue down the walkway and in the second-to-last cell is a coffin on your left you can open to find the Assassin's Set
  • Open the coffin on your right to find an Unknown Hero's Soul
  • Head out onto the walkway and on your right on the wall will be Prison of Hope, 4F Key + Prison of Hope, 4F Cell Key
  • Head back to the previous hall and head to the walkway on the other side. Open the first cell on your right to find an Unknown Hero's Soul, a Secret Dagger, and Claws
  • Go back out and raise the gate on your left. Here you can grab Fresh Spice x3
  • On your right a bit ahead is another gate you can raise. After that open the cell on your right to find the Once Royal Mistress
  • Head back out and on your right you can pick up a Renowned Hero's Soul
  • Go around to the other side and at the end of the walkway in the cell on your left will be three coffins containing a Ring of Magical Sharpness and two Unknown Hero's Soul
  • Lift the gate and head down the stairs. Just before the walkway you can pick up Old Spice
  • On your right will be Sage Freke, the Visionary. His cell can be unlocked once you collect the Prison of Hope, Special Key in a bit. Talk to him to receive Geri's Stiletto and to have him move to the Nexus
  • Go back into the stairwell and continue down. In the hall on your right you can pick up the Prison of Hope, 1F Key + Prison of Hope, 1F Cell Key
  • Continue forward and lift the gate. Head down the stairs and exit out onto the first walkway you see. Grab a Heavy Crossbow + Black Bolt x9
  • Ahead on this floor is Lord Rydell in the very last cell of the walkway. Depending on your World Tendency, you have options on what to do.
  • If you have Pure White World Tendency, Rydell can be rescued from his cell if you have the Prison of Hope, 2F W Cell Key, which can be collected from the Fool's Idol Archstone. Speak with him afterwards and he will give you the Dull Rat's Ring
  • If you have Pure White World Tendency, just beyond Rydell's cell is unbreakable debris that'll be gone now (it'll be present on any other World Tendency). In the first cell is the Venerable Sage's Set and a Three-Cornered Hat
  • If you have Pure White World Tendency, upon freeing Rydell and letting him live, you will find the Prison of Hope, 2F E Cell Key in the cells opposite of Rydell's cell. This key grants access to the East Wing of the 2nd Floor, that has cells that contain a Parrying Dagger, the Rogue's Set, and two Chunk of Mercurystone
  • If you have Pure Black World Tendency, Rydell will appear as a Black Phantom in front of his cell. He drops a Phosphorescent Pole
  • Continue down the stairs and open the gate at the bottom. Head out onto the walkway and open the second door on your left to pick up an Unknown Soldier's Soul
  • Continue to the end of the walkway to pick up the Silver Bracelets and a Fluted Helmet
  • Open the first door on your left as you head on to pick up an Unknown Soldier's Soul
  • At the end of the walkway by the Prisoner Horde will be Old Spice x3
  • Continue to the next hall and take a left. On your left in the third cell will be an Unknown Soldier's Soul
  • At the end of the walkway behind some coffins will be an open door. Head outside and go down the stairs to your left to pick up a Shard of Moonlightstone
  • Turn around and head forward until you can take a left, while still staying outside. At the end of the outside walkway among some rubble will be an Estoc
  • Go back inside and on the other walkway in the first cell to your right will be an Unknown Soldier's Soul
  • Further on the walkway will be a Crystal Lizard that drops Shard of Moonlightstone x3
  • By the open double gates will be a gate you can open
  • Head through the double gates and head head right. To your left through some coffins will be a broken fence you can walk through. Head along the ledge here to the end to grab a Stone of Ephemeral Eyes
  • Go back to the walkway and this time head the opposite direction. On your right at the end here will be a broken fence you can walk through onto a ledge. Head left and at the end of the ledge will be a Mercury Rapier+1
  • Head back up to the main walkway and continue to the start of the walkway with the Ballista. Grab the Prison of Hope, Warden's Key, which will be the first item you can grab here
  • Head all the way back to the stairwell with the two gates that we couldn't open earlier. Open the first gate on your left and down the walkway to your left will be a Renowned Hero's Soul
  • Head through the room with the double doors and on the walkway through here will be a Renowned Hero's Soul
  • Open the gate at the end of the walkway
  • Head back to the room with the double doors and open them. Head down the stairs and and enter the tower at the end. Head up the tower and head across the walkway to a door. Open the door to a hallway where at the end is a Silver Catalyst
  • Head back down the tower and pass through the fog gate. Pull the lever to deactivate the Ballista. Head across the walkway to pick up a Storied Soldier's Soul, the Clever Cat's Ring, a Renowned Soldier's Soul, another Renowned Soldier's Soul, and a Stone of Ephemeral Eyes
  • Turn around and just behind the arrow enemy to your right will be a tower. Head to the top and take a right. At the end of the walkway will be a door you can open to room with a Dregling you can talk to who plays innocent. Kill him so he will not be able to help the boss later
  • Head back down the walkway and to your right will be a broken fence. Drop down onto the arch to pick up Widow's Lotus x2
  • Drop down to the ground. You will be invaded by the Baby's Nail Black Phantom. Kill her and she will drop a Black Eye Stone + Chunk of Moonlightstone x3 + Stone of Ephemeral Eyes x2
  • Approach the massive doors for a cutscene. Pass through the fog gate and in the cathedral to your left behind you in the corner will be Old Spice x2
  • On the right side behind the third pillar will be a Storied Soldier's Soul
  • Defeat Fool's Idol
  • Pick up a Baby's Nail behind the altar, hugging the walls to avoid proceeding for now
  • Approach the altar directly to trigger a cutscene. Once you are transported by the Gargoyles, touch with the Fool's Idol Archstone to acquire the Doll Demon's Soul

Shrine of Storms

  • Touch the Archstone of the Shadowmen and warp to the Shrine of Storms
  • If you have Pure White World Tendency, Satsuki will appear at the entrance arch, looking for the Magic Sword "Makoto". Be careful giving it to Satsuki if you elect to, as giving/not giving/equipping the sword will result in Satsuki attacking you (if you gave him the sword, he will drop it upon death)
  • If you have Pure Black World Tendency, Satsuki will appear as a Black Phantom at the entrance arch. He will drop the Hiltless when killed
  • Head forward under the arch and to your left you can pick up Half Moon Grass x5
  • Head up the stairs and to your left at the top will be a Renowned Hero's Soul
  • Further to the left in the corner will be Soldier's Lotus x3
  • On the right side on a corpse against the wall will be a Talisman of God
  • Pass through the fog gate and head left through the tunnel. Take a left outside and to your right behind a tree will be Soul Remains x6
  • On the ledge behind the enemy will be a Crescent Falchion+1
  • Drop down and make your way make into the tunnel we just passed through. Head up to the rampart and at the end down the stairs will be the Copper Key
  • Go back across the rampart and head through the broken piece of wall. Continue through the tunnel with the Silver Skeleton and make your way up and out the door on your right outside. Up the first part will be an Augite of Guidance
  • Here you will also find Sparkly the Crow. Consult the Misc tab of this guide for information on available trades
  • To the right of Sparkly will be a drop-off area where you can grab a Stone of Ephemeral Eyes and kill a Crystal Lizard that drops Shard of Darkmoonstone x3
  • Make your way back up to the door that leads to Sparkly and head out onto the rampart with a Silver Skeleton and a Compound Long Bow + Light Arrow x13
  • Head back into the tower and at the staircase leading down from the base level is an illusory wall. Through this wall take a right at the fork with the two Silver Skeletons to find Crescent Moon Grass x4
  • At the end here is a cell that we can unlock to find Graverobber Blige and a Renowned Hero's Soul
  • Head back into the tunnel and head the other way of the fork to find another illusory wall. Go through it and at the top to the right of the fog gate will be an Uchigatana
  • To the left of the fog gate around the corner is an Unknown Hero's Soul
  • Head down the stairs here to find Shard of Cloudstone x2
  • Go back up and kill Vanguard for a Grey Demon's Soul
  • Here you can grab a Storied Hero's Soul
  • Near the well you can pick up a Kilij
  • A bit away from the well is Full Moon Grass x3
  • Head through the fog gate and to your left behind the Black Skeleton is an Adjudicator's Shield
  • Head past the Silver Skeleton archer and around the corner will be an Unknown Hero's Soul
  • Go back to the stairs and climb the ladder. Go across the bridge at the top to grab Half Moon Grass x2
  • Head back to the rampart with the two Silver Skeleton archers that we fought earlier. At the top of the tower, where we grabbed the Copper Key, you can roll over from the wooden platform onto the broken walls and drop down to pick up a Regenerator's Ring
  • Continue along the cliffside to kill a Crystal Lizard that drops Shard of Darkmoonstone x3
  • Under the archway to your right will be Shard of Cloudstone x2
  • Continue forward under the second archway to head up some stairs and grab Late Moon Grass x3
  • Head across a wooden beam here to pick up a Graverobber's Ring
  • Head back across and head over the ruined wall to find to your right a Renowned Hero's Soul
  • Head inside past the second archway and on the steps going down to a fog gate will be Half Moon Grass x3
  • Pass through the fog gate. From here you can pick up a Stone of Ephemeral Eyes and a Storied Soldier's Soul
  • Defeat Adjudicator; acquire Swollen Demon's Soul
  • Head down the stairs and behind the stairs on your left will be a Stone of Ephemeral Eyes
  • Behind the stairs on the right will be a Storied Soldier's Soul
  • Head into the hallway with Graverobber Blige and on your left will be Soul Remains x3

Valley of Defilement

  • Touch the Archstone of the Chieftain and warp to the Valley of Defilement
  • Head up the narrow wooden planks on your left and at the top on the right on a ledge will be a Renowned Hero's Soul
  • Drop down and stick to the right walkway. Drop down to the left of the falling boulders to pick up a Morning Star
  • Drop down a level and interact with the bridge to lower it
  • Head across to pick up a Talisman of God + Widow's Lotus x2
  • Drop down here and walk down a slope to grab an Unknown Hero's Soul
  • Head back and go across a walkway to a platform. Around the middle of the edge here you can drop down to pick up Crescent Moon Grass x3 and a Renowned Hero's Soul
  • Drop down through a hole here to pick up Crescent Moon Grass x2
  • Drop down another a level to find the Ring of Magical Dullness
  • Drop down again to grab a Dark Moon Grass
  • Descend the ladder and walk out on the overhanging plank. Carefully drop down into the haystack to avoid taking fall damage. Pick up a Stone of Ephemeral Eyes here
  • Head forward to pick up two Storied Soldier's Soul here
  • Kill the Crystal Lizard ahead for Shard of Faintstone x3
  • Ahead by the Giant Tick is a corpse with a Blessed Mace+1
  • Kick the bridge down here and pass through the fog gate. On your right will be Crescent Moon Grass
  • Head up the ramp to your left a bit to find the Filthy Woman
  • Continue up and head out on the ledge to pick up the Saint's Set + Stone of Ephemeral Eyes
  • Turn around and head to the right across the walkway into a room with a Shard of Faintstone and an Unknown Hero's Soul
  • Head back across the bridge and across the walkway to the Giant Depraved One. Make your way up and across another bridge with another Giant Depraved One. Descend a ramp here and at the bottom under the ramp will be Full Moon Grass x3
  • If you have Pure White World Tendency, there will be a ladder just before the bridge with the first Giant Depraved One. Climb the ladder and make your way down the path to find three Giant Depraved Ones guarding the Istarelle
  • Pass through the fog gate. Defeat Leechmonger; acquire Wriggling Demon's Soul
  • In this area you can pick up five Crescent Moon Grass x2, Crescent Moon Grass x5, two Shard of Faintstone, and a Chunk of Faintstone
  • Behind some planks here is Shard of Suckerstone x2

Phalanx Archstone

  • Touch the Archstone of the Small King and warp to the Phalanx Archstone
  • Head forward through the corridor and down the stairs. To your left here on a raised platform will be Half Moon Grass x3
  • Head up some stairs on your right and around halfway down will be Throwing Knife x10
  • Go back down and hug the left wall all the way into a ditch with an Unknown Hero's Soul
  • Go down the hall and head outside. On the bridge you can pick up Crescent Moon Grass, a Winged Spear, and Crescent Moon Grass x3
  • In the tower, head outside on your right and up the tower. At the top you can grab an Unknown Hero's Soul and a Wooden Catalyst
  • Kill the Red Dragon for a Small Flame-Scale Demon's Soul
  • Head down the stairs and then head down the tunnel. Past several Boletaria Soldiers is a Ring of Gash Resistance
  • Continue down all the way to the end to find Ostrava. Talk to him and he will give you Dark Moon Grass x3
  • Head back down the tunnel and continue past the stairs we descended. A bit past that on your left is an Unknown Hero's Soul
  • Continue past the Dogs to find Crescent Moon Grass x3 by the double gates
  • A bit further is the Dregling Merchant
  • To the side is a doorway with a staircase. Climb the stairs to the top of the tower to pick up a Compound Short Bow + Heavy Arrow x6
  • Head back down the stairs and go inside the tower and back out onto the bridge. A bit ahead by some Dreglings will be Crescent Moon Grass x2
  • Continue ahead on the bridge to pick up a Renowned Hero's Soul and a Spiked Shield
  • Forge ahead to pick up Crescent Moon Grass x3
  • Turn around and head back inside the tower. Pass through the fog gate and head on to find a Stone of Ephemeral Eyes
  • A bit further ahead is Full Moon Grass x2
  • Continue forward past the Blue Eye Knights and take a left to a passage with stairs. Climb the stairs find a Crystal Lizard that drops Large Sharpstone Shard x2
  • Continue up and outside to a rampart with two Boletaria Soldiers. Here you can pick up a Stone of Ephemeral Eyes and a Renowned Soldier's Soul
  • Here is also an Unknown Hero's Soul and Royal Lotus x3
  • Go back downstairs and pass through the fog gate. Climb the stairs on your right and in the passageway you can grab a Renowned Soldier's Soul
  • Continue forward past the Boletaria Soldiers to grab Late Moon Grass x3
  • Defeat Tower Knight; acquire Iron Demon's Soul

Armor Spider Archstone

  • Return to the Nexus, then touch the Archstone of the Burrow King and warp to the Armor Spider Archstone
  • Head down the tunnel and to your right will be a Crystal Lizard that drops Large Sharpstone Shard x2 + Shard of Clearstone x2
  • Head down the tunnel that the Crystal Lizard ran down and stick to the right onto a ledge. Roll through some debris and drop down three levels of wooden platforms. Drop off at the end with the corner against the wallto land on another platform. Stick to the wall and drop down another level. On a beam to your right is a Storied Soldier's Soul

FAQ

The foundation of this guide was based on Zachary Kjellberg's Dark Souls 3 Cheat Sheet code for use with IGN's Walkthrough Guide and xHolmesWalkthroughsx7's Demon's Souls Walkthrough

Is this a guide?

Not really. This is a set of checklists and information that you can use while playing Demon's Souls to make sure you don't miss an item, action, conversation, or boss. If you need a walkthrough, you can click on the zone titles to read the wiki walkthrough

Does this playthrough need to be followed in this exact order?

Demon's Souls is not a linear game and has multiple ways of progressing. This guide highlights zones as they become available, so feel free to follow however you please. You can always check things off in a different order

Can this be used for multiple characters?

Yes. Use the profile selector and buttons in the Options tab at the top of the page to setup multiple profiles

How does the checklist status get saved?

The checklist is saved to your browser's local storage. Be careful when clearing your browser's cache as it will also destroy your saved progress
